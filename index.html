Aloe Pencil

Our main ingredient is Aloe vera.It's an evergreen plant that grows wild in warm, subtropical, arid climates throughout the world. It is cultivated for commercial products, mostly as a topical medicine, and has been used for centuries. It is used in many foods, beverages, lotions, cosmetics, ointments or as a gel for minor burns and sunburns. We used it to replace de wood of the pencil to avoid the felling of trees, and mix its savila with graphite to reduce the production of it. The benefits of consuming our product its that you are ahieving responsable consumption, and this pencil doesn´t support the felling of trees.
